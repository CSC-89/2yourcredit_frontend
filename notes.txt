

Calculation for determining monthly amount to pay:

P = premium
r = interest rate / periods in year
n = periods in year * years

P(r * ((1+r)^n)) / (((1+r)^n) -1) 

example: 

P = 10000
r = 0.075 / 12 (0.00625)
n = 12 * 5 (60)


30000(0.00625 * (1.00625^60)) / ((1.00625^60) - 1)

////

This does not include initiation cost or monthly fees.
